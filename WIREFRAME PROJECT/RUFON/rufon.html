Short story of Myself

Hello! I`m Joart Virtudazo Rufon and I am currently studying at Technological University of the Philippines-Manila with the program
of Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering. I am the oldest child of Annie and Mariano Rufon. Growing up my life has a lot of difficulties, but I cannot tell because it is personal. I was born and raised in the province of Agusan Del Sur, specifically in the town of Prosperidad in Mindanao. My favourite fruit is Durian where every year the durian tree in our backyard always has fruit. When I was a kid, I used to play kariling. This game only requires an old bicycle tire, I play it with my childhood friends in my province. I came up to study here in Metro-Manila when I was 14 years old when my auntie brings me here in this Industrious City to support my family education, I was grade 8 that time and it is hard for me to speak tagalog because in my whole life I only speak bisaya. Back then, I am a cool person that do not usually interact to my classmates but day by day as my surroundings talk tagalog I able to speak tagalog as well. I graduated from my junior high school and senior high school in Navotas National High School where in junior high school I received with honor and in senior high I received with high honor. After I graduated in senior high, I interested to become a soldier and enter in Philippine Military Academy unfortunately I did not pass the entrance exam. So that my second choice to be an engineer led me here.
